About

The Citizen Historian Sourcebook is a plattform for primary source documents and learning resources across a range of humanities fields and historical periods. In addition to a selection of source material, the CHS enables researchers, teachers, and students to deepen their understanding of historical topics. Editorial notes and further reading recommendations contextualize documents and connect researchers with current literature in the field.

The CHS is a digital history initiative by the New England Journal of History and Dean College.
